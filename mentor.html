FEMI OTEDOLA


Femi Otedola is a Nigerian businessman, entrepreneur, and philanthropist. He was born on November 4, 1962, in Ibadan, Nigeria. Otedola is the founder of Zenon Petroleum and Gas Ltd, a prominent oil marketing and distribution company in Nigeria. He is also the former chairman of Forte Oil PLC, an oil and gas distribution company in Nigeria.

In addition to his business ventures, Otedola is known for his philanthropy and has donated millions of dollars to charitable causes. He has supported a wide range of initiatives, including the construction of schools and hospitals, and has also donated to various disaster relief efforts.

Otedola's success in business has earned him numerous accolades, including being named the second richest man in Nigeria by Forbes in 2015. He is a prominent figure in Nigeria's business and political spheres and continues to be an influential voice in shaping the country's economic landscape.

GEORGE SOROS

George Soros is a Hungarian-American investor, philanthropist, and political activist. Born in Budapest, Hungary, in 1930, Soros survived the Nazi occupation of Hungary and later immigrated to England in 1947. He attended the London School of Economics and graduated with a degree in philosophy in 1952. After working various jobs, Soros began his finance career in 1954, working for several firms before starting his own hedge fund, Soros Fund Management, in 1970.

Soros gained notoriety in the 1990s for his high-profile currency trades, earning him the nickname "The Man Who Broke the Bank of England." He has also been a prominent philanthropist and has donated billions of dollars to various causes, including promoting democracy and human rights around the world. Soros is also known for his political activism and has been a major donor to various political campaigns and causes. He is a strong advocate for progressive causes and has supported various Democratic candidates and organizations in the United States.

Despite controversy and criticism over the years, Soros remains a significant figure in the worlds of finance, philanthropy, and politics, and his impact on global affairs continues to be felt to this day.
