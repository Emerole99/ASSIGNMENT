MY GOALS FOR ATTENDING THIS COURSE

My goals for attending the web development course in HiiT is to create websites and web applications that are aesthetically pleasing, easy to use, and secure. I aim to have an understanding of both the front-end and back-end of web development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, databases, and networking. A major motivation is self-expression in being able to design and develop a website to be proud of. The main objective when working as a developer is to be productive and deliver value to the company which the service is being provided to & for the proposed audience to be able to navigate the site easily.

I am passionate about studying web development for several reasons. First, I recognize the increasing importance of technology in our daily lives and see web development as an exciting and dynamic field that offers endless possibilities for innovation and creativity. Second, I am fascinated by the potential to create engaging and interactive user experiences on the web, and I want to develop the skills necessary to build visually stunning and user-friendly websites. Third, I believe that web development is a valuable skill that is in high demand in today's job market, and I hope to build a career in the field that allows me to continue learning and growing while making a meaningful impact.
